Video Game Ontology Domains

Game Genres

Categories that define the style and type of a video game.
Genres range from Action, RPG, Puzzle, Simulation, to Sports.
They help players quickly understand gameplay expectations.
Genres provide the foundation for narrative, mechanics, and pacing.

Platforms

The hardware or system where games are played,
including PC, Console, Mobile, and Virtual Reality.
Platform choice impacts controls, graphics, and performance.
Exclusive games often define platform popularity.

Player Interaction

How players engage with games.
This includes single-player, multiplayer, or co-op modes.
Interactions involve mechanics like exploration, crafting, or combat.
The balance of challenge and reward shapes the overall experience.
