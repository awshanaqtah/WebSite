EA SPORTS FC 24 (formerly FIFA 24)

• Genre: Sports (Football/Soccer)
• Platforms: PS4, PS5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X|S, Nintendo Switch, PC
• Developer: EA Sports
• Publisher: Electronic Arts
• Release Date: September 29, 2023
• Game Modes: Single-player, Multiplayer, Co-op, Ultimate Team, MyCAREER, Clubs, VOLTA Football
• Rating: ESRB E (Everyone)
EA SPORTS FC 24 marks the beginning of a new era for football gaming. With over 19,000 fully licensed players, 700 teams, and 30 leagues—including the UEFA Men's and Women's Champions League—it offers unparalleled authenticity. The game introduces three cutting-edge technologies: HyperMotionV, PlayStyles optimized by Opta, and the enhanced Frostbite™ Engine, delivering lifelike detail and immersive gameplay. A post-launch update at no additional cost brings the excitement of UEFA EURO 2024 to the game.
